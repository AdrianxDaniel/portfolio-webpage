About Me

I consider myself a dynamic individual with a wide range of interests and a resilient attitude towards life's challenges. Despite facing setbacks in my academic journey, such as not being accepted into my school's Nursing Program due to its limited spots and a huge applicant pool, I've always remained determined and focused on my goals. My educational background spans across Criminal Justice and Nursing, and I've gained valuable experience in the logistics industry over the past eight years. In my current role, I take pride in meticulously managing logistics operations, ensuring smooth cargo flow, and addressing any issues that arise along the way. However, beyond my professional endeavors, my true passion lies in the realm of creativity and technology. I often find myself immersed in brainstorming ideas for games and contemplating ways to enhance existing ones. This passion has led me to embark on a journey to learn programming, as I believe it holds the key to realizing my dreams of bringing my imaginative ideas to life. Furthermore, I've always viewed myself as someone with a strong temperament. While some may perceive a strong temper as easily getting agitated, I see it as the ability to maintain composure and channel emotions into productive outcomes. This trait has helped me navigate life's ups and downs with resilience and grace.
